# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Students learning from dense PDF lecture material on desktop and Android. They need fast, focused recall practice, visible learning progress, and resumable sessions.

## Product Purpose

denisLearn converts a PDF into exam-focused Anki-style cards, groups them by category, and provides a mobile PWA for learning. Success means the user can pick categories, set a session size, answer sequential cards, and have progress persist locally until every card is confirmed correct three times.

## Brand Personality

Focused, precise, calm.

## Anti-references

Avoid toy flashcard UI, decorative landing pages, gamified clutter, and dashboards that hide the next learning action behind marketing copy.

## Design Principles

1. Make the next study action obvious.
2. Keep learning state readable at a glance.
3. Treat mobile as the primary surface.
4. Preserve source context for hard cards.
5. Keep controls standard and predictable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use high-contrast text, keyboard-reachable controls, clear focus states, reduced-motion fallbacks, and no color-only progress signals.
